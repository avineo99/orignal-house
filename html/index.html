About Us

Original House was set up by Archie and Caddy Mackie in 2008.

Born from a passion for design and an appreciation for quality materials and craftmanship.

From humble beginings working in the world of architectural reclamation.

Over the years we have refined our style, proudly sourcing unique pieces for our diserning customers. more recently we have taken the lessons learnt over years of restoring and are producing our own style of handmade and painted furniture. a family business, we love what we do.

Pieces that have patina and charm that only time and ware can create. A move away from more traditional antiques towards a more laid-back feel. Our antique and bespoke pieces fit well not just within period interiors but contrast beautifully with the clean lines of modern spaces. Often our pieces are used as statment pieces but in a subtle understated fashion. Bringing a splash of history, colour and texture. Whether Mid-century, Vintage, Industrial, Folk Art, Baroque or Georgian we follow no rules and love mixing styles.

We have a showroom in the Cotswolds full of French,

Italian, Spanish and English Antiques.
